Meet Dr. Abhishek Tiwari

Dr. Abhishek Tiwari is a passionate pediatrician and active parent coach who has dedicated his life to supporting parents and children on their journey of growth. From his early dream of becoming a pediatrician to his transformation into a trusted parent coach, his journey reflects both personal experience and professional expertise.

With more than 25,000 parents mentored in the last 15+ years, Dr. Tiwari and his team have touched countless lives by providing practical solutions, compassionate advice, and modern parenting insights. His mission is to empower every parent to raise resilient, confident, and happy children while creating a harmonious family life.
